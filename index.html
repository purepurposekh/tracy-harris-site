About Tracy

I see you.

I'm a former teacher turned entrepreneur, a mum of two boys, and the founder of a multi-seven-figure digital education company.

I built this business while living between Australia and Bali. While working four days a week. While prioritising my health, my family, and my faith.

I didn't build it fast. I built it right.

And now I help other women do the same. Not with hype. Not with hustle culture. With strategy, self-leadership, and a framework I call F.R.E.S.H.

The Framework

F.R.E.S.H.

My personal operating system for building a business that feeds your life instead of consuming it. Every letter, every day.
F
Fitness
Moving your body from a place of self-care and energy, not guilt or punishment.
R
Relationships
Surrounding yourself with people who are aligned with where you're going, not just where you've been.
E
Environment
Creating a workspace and daily life that feels intentional, energising, and aligned.
S
Self
Trusting your instincts, investing in personal growth, and leading yourself first.
H
Hustle
Doing the work with purpose and strategy, not burnout and busyness.
