About Tracy

Former teacher.
Founder. Leader.

I built a multi-seven-figure digital education company while living between Australia and Bali. While working four days a week. While raising two boys. While prioritising my health, my faith, and my marriage.

I didn't build it fast. I built it right.

Now I help women do the same. Not with hype. Not with hustle culture. With real strategy, deep self-leadership, and a framework called F.R.E.S.H. that treats your life and your business as one integrated system.

Because the women I work with don't want to choose between revenue and rest. They want both. And they should have both.

The Framework

F.R.E.S.H.

My personal operating system for building a business that feeds your life instead of consuming it.

F
Fitness
Move your body from a place of self-care and energy. Not guilt. Not punishment. The foundation of everything.
R
Relationships
Surround yourself with people aligned to where you're going. Not just where you've been.
E
Environment
Your workspace, your home, your daily rhythm. Make it intentional. Make it energising.
S
Self
Trust your instincts. Invest in your growth. Lead yourself before you lead others.
H
Hustle
Do the work with strategy and purpose. Not burnout and busyness. Smart hustle, not hard hustle.
