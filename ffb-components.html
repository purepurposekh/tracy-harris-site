Section: faq-s

QUESTIONS

Who is this mentorship for?

This mentorship is for serious female digital entrepreneurs who are ready to stop consuming endless information and start implementing effective strategies. It's for those who value work-life harmony, want to build a freedom-filled business, and are committed to growth.

Who is this mentorship not for?

This program is not for information hoarders, quick-fix seekers, complainers, rigid thinkers, solitary operators, or excuse-makers. If you're not ready to take responsibility for your growth and embrace change, this isn't the right fit.

What topics will be covered in the mentorship?

We will cover a range of topics including business strategy, work-life harmony, leadership, offer development, launch strategies, mindset mastery, and more. Plus, you'll receive exclusive bonuses like the FRESH Framework and Tracy's Take sessions.

How do I join the program?

To join, click the link below to secure your spot. Begin your journey to a freedom-filled business and life.

How long is the program?

The mentorship lasts for one year, allowing for deep, meaningful integration and growth. This ensures you have ample time to implement the strategies and see real results.

What if I fall behind?

This program is designed for relaxed integration, so you can move at your own pace without the pressure of falling behind. We believe in sustainable progress, not rushed timelines.

How is this different from other mentorship programs?

Freedom-Filled™ Business is a mentorship with mastermind elements. It's not a typical program where you're forced into a rigid structure or niche. Instead, we focus on personalised guidance, practical implementation, and building a business that aligns with your unique strengths and values.

What kind of support will I receive?

You'll have access to monthly group coaching calls, Freedom-Maker Circles (mini mastermind groups), and a resource library. Plus, you'll be part of an exclusive, elevated community on Circle designed for deep connection and support.

What is the investment and payment options?

The mentorship will be offered at $6,000 AUD per year. There are three payment options available: pay in full for $6,000 AUD a 12-month payment plan at $500 AUD per month or a 6-pay option at $1,000 over 6 consecutive payments. A commitment for 12 months is required.

Can I still get support if I have questions outside of group sessions?

Yes, our community platform on Circle is designed for ongoing support. You can ask questions, share experiences, and get feedback from both me and your peers throughout the program.
