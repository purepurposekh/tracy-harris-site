Meet Tracy

I Refused to Believe
This Was All There Was

I was a teacher, a mum, and completely burnt out. I loved my boys, but I had lost myself somewhere between school drop-off and marking papers at midnight.

So I started building. First an Instagram account from my kitchen table. Then a podcast. Then a course. Then a multi-seven-figure education business that serves thousands of women around the world.

Today I work four days a week, live between Australia and Bali, and pour my energy into helping other women create businesses that give them freedom, not just revenue.

My Operating System

The F.R.E.S.H. Framework

F

Freedom

Your business should give you more life, not less. We design around the hours, the energy, and the lifestyle you actually want.

R

Revenue

Freedom without revenue is a hobby. We build clean, profitable business models that scale without burning you out.

E

Engagement

Real connection with your audience. Not vanity metrics, not algorithm hacks. Women who trust you and want to work with you.

S

Strategy

Every decision anchored in a plan. Your content, your offers, your launches, all moving in the same direction with intention.

H

Heart

The work matters. The women you serve matter. Building with integrity, leading with love, and creating something you are genuinely proud of.
